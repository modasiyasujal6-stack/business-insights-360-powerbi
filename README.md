# Business Insights 360 - FMCG Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

Business Insights 360 is a multi-functional Power BI dashboard developed during the Codebasics Power BI learning program.

The project focuses on analyzing business performance across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, Executive, and Product domains for a fictional FMCG company.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators, identify growth opportunities, evaluate profitability, improve forecasting accuracy, and make data-driven business decisions.

## Business Problem

Management teams often struggle to obtain a unified view of business performance across multiple departments.

This dashboard consolidates finance, sales, marketing, supply chain, and product performance into a single reporting solution, helping stakeholders:

- Monitor revenue and profitability
- Analyze customer and product performance
- Track forecast accuracy
- Identify operational inefficiencies
- Evaluate regional and market performance
